Translation of sign language to other languages

Translation services where the recognized sign language could be translated to other spoken languages or text in real-time. This would enable the deaf or hard-of-hearing to communicate with people who do not understand Indian sign language.

Customization of the software

Provides customization services where users could customize the software to their specific needs, for example, by adding new signs or gestures that are unique to their region or community.

Integration with other software

Integrates Indian sign language recognition software with other software and devices, such as smartphones or computers, to enhance accessibility for the deaf and hard-of-hearing. For example, the software could be integrated with video conferencing software to enable deaf individuals to participate in online meetings.

Training and support for users

Offers training and support services to users, such as individuals, schools, and organizations, to help them effectively use the software and troubleshoot any issues that may arise. This would ensure that the software is being used to its full potential and that users are able to communicate more effectively using Indian sign language.

FEATURED WORK

Sign language dictionary creation

Provides a service to create a comprehensive sign language dictionary for Indian sign language, based on the data collected from the recognition software. This dictionary could be used by schools, organizations, and individuals to learn and understand Indian sign language more effectively. Additionally, offers ongoing updates and maintenance to ensure the dictionary stays current with any new or evolving signs.
